5 Fun Facts

  • Originally they were called the Boston Patriots, before they later changed their name to the New England Patriots
  • The New England Patriots has the longest active consecutive NFL playoff appearance streak, for 10 seasons
  • Tom Brady, the superstar QB(quarterback) of the New England Patriots was drafted in the 6th round as a fourth string QB
  • In 2007 the New England Patriots won all 16 of their regular season games
  • The New England Patriots have the most Super Bowl out of all the NFL teams, they've won 5 and lost 5 and are playing in their 11th on February 3rd
