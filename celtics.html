5 Fun Facts

  • The Boston Celtics are the most successful NBA franchise in the history of the NBA with 17 Championships
  • In 1950 the Celtics drafted Chuck Cooper, who was the first African American player in the NBA
  • The Boston Celtics won 8 consecutive championships from 1959 to 1966
  • Their mascot Lucky the Leprechaun was chosen with regards to the large Irish population in Boston
  • The Boston Celtics are one of two NBA franchises to never move cities, the other being the New York Knicks
